
Operators:
Arithmetic Operators: Addition (+), subtraction (-), multiplication (*), division (/), modulus (%), aur exponentiation (**).
Assignment Operators: Assignment (=), addition assignment (+=), subtraction assignment (-=), multiplication assignment (*=), division assignment (/=), modulus assignment (%=).
Comparison Operators: Equal to (==), not equal to (!=), strict equal to (===), strict not equal to (!==), greater than (>), less than (<), greater than or equal to (>=), less than or equal to (<=).
Logical Operators: AND (&&), OR (||), NOT (!).
Unary Operators: Increment (++), decrement (--).
Ternary Operator: Conditional operator (condition ? expr1 : expr2).
